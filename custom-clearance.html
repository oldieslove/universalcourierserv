Customs clearance is one of the most crucial components in cargo shipment as your advantage of speed to market can be lost if goods lie in docks uncleared even for a day. The Customs documentation process also demands a great deal of precision. Incomplete or wrong customs documentation can lead to considerable delays in movement of cargo and even cost overruns,

At Universal Courier Services the entire process of Customs clearance is handled by a group of professionals with the requisite skills and knowledge to fast-track your shipments through Customs. We also ensure that our clients are updated real-time on the status of their shipments.
